About Me

I am a marketer and aspiring full-stack developer based in San Francisco, California. Born and raised in the East Bay, I consider myself a Bay Area native despite only recently returning to the Bay after 20 years away from home.

I have a BA in Chinese Language and Literature from UC Irvine and an MBA from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). Upon receiving my MBA, I was recruited by AXA, a French multi-national insurance company, where I held numerous management positions across various functions over the past decade. Most recently, I was the Head of Marketing for AXA in Indonesia.

In a past life (i.e. prior to fatherhood), I was an avid traveler and beach connoisseur. My three favorite beaches, in no particular order, are Whitehaven Beach on Whitsunday Island, Australia, Furuzamami Beach on Zamami Island, Okinawa, and Phra Nang Beach in Krabi, Thailand.
